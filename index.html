A Baytown Institution

We've been on East Texas Avenue since 1974. That's over fifty years of cutting hair in the same town, the same way.

Fathers bring their sons to us now, same as their fathers did before them. The chairs have changed. The scissors haven't. Well, the scissors have, but you know what we mean.

"This is a uniquely classic barber shop with all the quirks you'd expect."

We're not trying to be fancy. We're just trying to give you a good haircut at a fair price. That's been the plan since day one, and we don't see why that would change.
